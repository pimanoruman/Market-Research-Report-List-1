<p><p>Aluminium dross recycling involves processing waste generated during the aluminium production process. The market is categorized into primary and secondary aluminium dross. Primary aluminium dross is produced during the initial refining of aluminum; it contains a higher metal content and is usually more valuable for recycling. In contrast, secondary aluminium dross arises from recycled aluminium processing and typically has a lower metal content. Both types are essential for reducing waste and recovering valuable aluminium resources, promoting environmental sustainability.</p></p>
