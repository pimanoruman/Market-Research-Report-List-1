<p><p>The Inorganic Thin-Film Encapsulation Layer market comprises various types of technologies, primarily focusing on PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) and ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition). PECVD is favored for its ability to produce high-quality films at lower temperatures, making it ideal for sensitive substrates. ALD offers precise thickness control and uniform coverage at the nanoscale, enhancing barrier properties. Other methods may include sputtering and chemical vapor deposition, catering to diverse applications in electronics, displays, and flexible devices.</p></p>
