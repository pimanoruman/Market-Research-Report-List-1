<p><ul><li>Straddle Monorail</li><li>Suspended Monorail</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monorail systems can be categorized into different types based on their design and operational characteristics. Straddle monorails feature a beam that supports the train from below, allowing for minimal ground disruption and efficient urban transit. In contrast, suspended monorails hang from a rail overhead, providing a unique perspective and facilitating movement in densely populated areas. Both types contribute to reducing traffic congestion and offer eco-friendly transportation solutions while being suitable for diverse environments, from urban centers to amusement parks.</p></p>
