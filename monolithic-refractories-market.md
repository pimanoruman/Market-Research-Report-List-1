<p><p>Monolithic refractories are unshaped refractory materials that are installed as a single mass, offering flexibility and adaptability in high-temperature applications. Unlike traditional brick refractories, they are applied in a plastic or semi-fluid state, allowing for seamless installation and repair in complex structures.
