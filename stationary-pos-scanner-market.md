<p><p>The stationary POS scanner market comprises three main types: presentation scanners, bioptic scanners, and mini-slot scanners. Presentation scanners are designed for quick, hands-free scanning, ideal for retail environments with high transaction volumes. Bioptic scanners combine handheld and fixed scanning, allowing for flexible use in various settings, including grocery stores. Mini-slot scanners, compact and space-efficient, are suited for limited counter space, typically used in convenience stores or pharmacies, offering reliable scanning capabilities while optimizing available area.</p></p>
