<p><p>Stackers are crucial in material handling, categorized into different types based on functionality and power source. Pallet stackers are designed for lifting and stacking palletized loads. Manual stackers require operator effort to lift loads, while hydraulic stackers utilize hydraulic systems for easier lifting. Electric stackers offer powered lifting and movement for larger loads. Semi-electric stackers combine manual base operation with electric lifting, providing a balance of control and power. Together, these stacker types enhance efficiency in various warehouse and industrial environments.</p></p>
