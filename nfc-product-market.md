<p><strong>The NFC Product Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NFC Controller</li><li>NFC Tag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NFC product market comprises primarily two segments: NFC controllers and NFC tags. NFC controllers are integrated circuits that facilitate communication between devices, often found in smartphones and payment terminals, enabling secure transactions and data exchange. In contrast, NFC tags are passive components that store data and can be read by NFC-enabled devices when in proximity, widely used in applications like product labeling, access control, and marketing. Together, they enhance connectivity and customer engagement across various industries.</p></p>
