<p><p>NdFeB permanent magnets, composed of neodymium, iron, and boron, are categorized into two main types: sintered and bonded NdFeB magnets. Sintered NdFeB magnets are produced through a sintering process, yielding high magnetic strength and stability, making them ideal for industrial applications. Bonded NdFeB magnets, created by mixing magnet powder with a polymer binder and then forming it, offer flexibility in shapes and sizes, catering to diverse applications in consumer electronics and automotive industries.</p></p>
