<p><p>The quench tank market comprises three main types: oil quench tanks, water quench tanks, and air quench tanks. Oil quench tanks utilize heating oils for rapid cooling, providing excellent thermal stability and reducing distortion in treated materials. Water quench tanks are commonly used for their cost-effectiveness and efficient heat transfer, suitable for various metals. Air quench tanks offer a non-invasive cooling method, ideal for heat-sensitive components. Each type serves distinct industrial applications based on cooling requirements and material properties.</p></p>
