<p><p>The Wet-End Paper Making Starches market encompasses two primary types: Wet End Starch and Wet End Cationic Starch. Wet End Starch is utilized to enhance paper strength and improve retention during the papermaking process, promoting better fiber bonding. Wet End Cationic Starch, on the other hand, is specifically designed for its positively charged properties, which aid in improved fine fiber retention and the building of surface strength in paper products. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing paper quality and production efficiency.</p></p>
