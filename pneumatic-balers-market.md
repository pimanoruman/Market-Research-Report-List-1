<p><p>The pneumatic balers market comprises primarily two types: vertical balers and horizontal balers. Vertical balers operate by compressing materials from the top down, making them space-efficient for smaller operations and ideal for various waste materials. Horizontal balers, on the other hand, compress materials from the sides and are suited for larger-scale operations, capable of handling higher volumes and varying material types. Both types play crucial roles in waste management and recycling, enhancing efficiency and reducing landfill waste.</p></p>
