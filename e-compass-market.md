<p><p>E-Compass market types include Fluxgate, Hall-Effect, Magneto Resistive, and Others. Fluxgate sensors detect changes in magnetic fields for precise navigation and orientation. Hall-Effect sensors operate on the principle of generating a voltage in response to a magnetic field, commonly found in automotive applications. Magneto Resistive sensors utilize resistance changes in materials under magnetic influence, offering high sensitivity. The "Others" category encompasses emerging technologies and specialized applications, reflecting the diverse needs of industries like consumer electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
