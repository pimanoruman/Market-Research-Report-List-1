<p><p>The Towline Floor Conveyor market is categorized into two main types: on-floor and in-floor towline conveyors. On-floor towline conveyors operate at the surface level, utilizing a track system to transport materials efficiently across a facility. In contrast, in-floor towline conveyors are embedded within the flooring, allowing for a seamless and unobtrusive transport solution, ideal for high-density production environments. Both types are designed to enhance productivity and streamline workflow by facilitating the movement of goods in various industrial settings.</p></p>
