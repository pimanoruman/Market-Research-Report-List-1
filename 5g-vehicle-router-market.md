<p><p>The 5G Vehicle Router market is categorized into Single Card and Dual Card types. Conversely, Dual Card routers accommodate two SIM cards, providing enhanced reliability and failover capabilities, which are essential for critical applications requiring uninterrupted connectivity. This dual setup allows for greater bandwidth and redundancy, catering to more demanding user needs in various vehicle applications.</p></p>
