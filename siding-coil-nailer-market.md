<p><p>The siding coil nailer market is primarily divided into two types: pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic siding coil nailers use compressed air as a power source, offering high speed and efficiency for large projects. They are favored for their lightweight design and consistent performance. On the other hand, electric siding coil nailers are powered by electricity, either via a corded connection or rechargeable batteries. They provide more portability and are quieter during operation but may have limitations in power compared to pneumatic models.</p></p>
