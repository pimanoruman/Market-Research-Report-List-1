<p><p>The portable respirators market comprises several types designed for different applications. Fixed volume type respirators deliver a predetermined amount of air, suitable for specific tasks. Timing type respirators provide air based on a preset duration, ideal for controlled operations. Mixed-type respirators combine features of both fixed volume and constant pressure systems, offering versatility for a wide range of industrial or emergency scenarios.</p></p>
