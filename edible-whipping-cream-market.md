<p><p>The edible whipping cream market comprises various types, each catering to different culinary needs. Light whipping cream typically contains around 30% fat, ideal for topping desserts and light dishes. Heavy whipping cream, with about 36% fat, offers a richer texture suitable for baking and sauces. Double cream, featuring over 48% fat, provides an exceptionally thick consistency, perfect for luxurious desserts. Manufacturing cream is specially formulated for food production, offering consistent quality and high fat content for use in processed foods and commercial kitchens.</p></p>
