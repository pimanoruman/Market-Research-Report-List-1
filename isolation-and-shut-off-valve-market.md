<p><p>The isolation and shut-off valve market encompasses various types designed to control fluid flow in pipelines. Medium-operation valves are suited for moderate pressure and temperature applications, ensuring reliable operation. Gate valves provide a straight-through flow with minimal resistance, ideal for on/off control. Check valves prevent backflow, maintaining the direction of fluid flow. Globe valves offer precise flow regulation, making them suitable for throttling applications. Other types include ball valves and butterfly valves, expanding the market's offerings for specific operational needs.</p></p>
