<p><p>The Pantograph Carbon Strips market features primarily two types: metal-impregnated carbon strips and pure graphite carbon strips. Metal-impregnated carbon strips are enhanced with metals to improve conductivity and wear resistance, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, pure graphite carbon strips offer excellent lubricating properties and thermal stability, ideal for environments with high temperatures and demanding electrical conditions. Both types are essential in various industries, including railways, electric vehicles, and industrial machinery, contributing to efficient energy transfer and equipment longevity.</p></p>
