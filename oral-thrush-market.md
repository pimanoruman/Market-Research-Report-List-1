<p><strong>The Oral Thrush Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pseudomembranous</li><li>Erythematous</li><li>Hyperplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oral thrush is categorized into three main types based on its presentation. Pseudomembranous thrush features white, detachable lesions on the mucous membranes, often causing discomfort. Erythematous thrush, on the other hand, appears as red, inflamed patches, typically on the tongue or oral cavity, and can be painful. Hyperplastic thrush is a less common variant characterized by thick, white plaques that do not easily rub off, and may indicate more severe underlying issues. Each type reflects distinct clinical manifestations and implications for treatment.</p></p>
