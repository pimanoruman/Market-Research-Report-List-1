<p><strong>The Reflow Soldering Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Ovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reflow soldering oven market features two primary types: convection ovens and vapor phase ovens. Convection ovens use heated air circulation to evenly distribute warmth, ensuring consistent soldering by melting solder paste on printed circuit boards. In contrast, vapor phase ovens employ a vaporized liquid to create a controlled thermal environment, allowing for uniform heating without direct contact. Both technologies are essential in electronics manufacturing, each offering unique advantages for precision soldering and efficient production processes.</p></p>
