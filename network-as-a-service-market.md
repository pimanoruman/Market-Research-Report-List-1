<p><p>Network-as-a-Service (NaaS) encompasses various market segments, including LANaaS and WANaaS. LANaaS focuses on providing localized network infrastructure, offering organizations scalable, on-demand access to local area networks without the need for extensive hardware investments. Conversely, WANaaS delivers wide area network services, enabling businesses to establish and manage connectivity across geographically dispersed sites efficiently. Both models promote flexibility, cost savings, and easier management, empowering organizations to adapt network resources to evolving business requirements.</p></p>
