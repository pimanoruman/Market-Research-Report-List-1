<p><p>The Encapsulated Salt Market comprises three main types: Solar Salt, Rock Salt, and Vacuum Salt. Solar Salt is derived from evaporating seawater and is often used in food processing and industrial applications. Rock Salt, harvested from underground salt deposits, is utilized primarily for de-icing and certain culinary uses. Vacuum Salt is produced through evaporation in a vacuum, ensuring high purity levels and is commonly employed in pharmaceuticals and specialty food products. Each type caters to distinct industry needs and applications.</p></p>
