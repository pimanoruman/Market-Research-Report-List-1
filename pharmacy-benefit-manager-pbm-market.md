<p><p>Pharmacy Benefit Managers (PBMs) operate in two primary market types: Type I and Type II. In a Type I market, PBMs negotiate directly with pharmaceutical manufacturers and pharmacies, aligning their interests to manage drug costs and access efficiently. Conversely, Type II markets involve PBMs primarily serving health plans and employers, managing benefits and controlling expenditures without direct negotiation with manufacturers. Both types aim to optimize medication access and affordability while navigating complex relationships within the healthcare system.</p></p>
