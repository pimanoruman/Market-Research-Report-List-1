<p><strong>The PoE Connected Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powered Device Controllers and Ics</li><li>Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers and Ics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PoE (Power over Ethernet) connected lighting market includes two main categories: Powered Device (PD) Controllers and Integrated Circuits (ICs) and Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controllers and ICs. PD Controllers manage the functionality of lighting fixtures that receive power through Ethernet cables, enabling features like dimming and color control. PSE Controllers facilitate power management and distribution from a network switch or injector to multiple powered devices, ensuring efficient energy use, and simplifying installation and cabling for lighting systems.</p></p>
