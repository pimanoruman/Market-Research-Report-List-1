<p><p>The Smart Coating market is categorized based on responsive mechanisms like pH, ionic strength, temperature, pressure, and other stimuli. pH-sensitive coatings change properties with acidity or alkalinity shifts. Ionic strength-responsive coatings adapt to variations in ion concentration. Temperature-sensitive coatings react to thermal changes, while pressure-responsive coatings adjust under mechanical stress. Other stimuli include light, humidity, or electric fields. These coatings are used in industries like healthcare, automotive, and construction for self-healing, anti-corrosion, and energy efficiency applications.</p></p>
