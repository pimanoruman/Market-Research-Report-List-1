<p><p>The Collaborative Warehouse Robotics market comprises two main types: mobile robotics and fixed robotics. Mobile robotics involve autonomous vehicles designed for flexible navigation within the warehouse, enhancing efficiency by transporting goods and managing inventory in dynamic environments. Meanwhile, fixed robotics refers to stationary systems that undertake repetitive tasks, such as picking and packing, often integrated into conveyor systems. Both types enhance operational productivity, reduce labor costs, and improve safety through collaboration with human workers in warehouse settings.</p></p>
