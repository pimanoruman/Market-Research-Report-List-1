<p><p>The disposable gloves market includes three main types: latex, vinyl, and nitrile. Latex gloves, made from natural rubber, offer excellent flexibility and tactile sensitivity but may cause allergies in some users. Vinyl gloves, derived from synthetic materials, are economical and suitable for low-risk situations, though they provide less durability and barrier protection. Nitrile gloves, crafted from synthetic rubber, are highly resistant to punctures, chemicals, and provide a snug fit, making them ideal for medical and industrial applications.</p></p>
