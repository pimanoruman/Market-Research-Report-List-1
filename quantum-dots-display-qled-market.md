The QLED market is categorized mainly into two types: QDEF (Quantum Dot Enhancement Film), which improves the performance of traditional LED displays by augmenting their color range and brightness, and direct QLED displays, which integrate quantum dots with an LED backlight for superior image quality. Both types offer advancements in display performance, appealing to consumers and industries alike.</p></p>
