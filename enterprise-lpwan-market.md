<p><p>The Enterprise LPWAN market encompasses both Cellular-Based and Non-Cellular LPWAN technologies. Cellular-Based LPWAN leverages existing cellular infrastructure, utilizing standards like LTE-M and NB-IoT, ensuring wide coverage and reliability for IoT applications. In contrast, Non-Cellular LPWAN operates independently from cellular networks, employing technologies like LoRaWAN and Sigfox, which are suited for low-power, long-range communications, typically in rural or remote areas. Both types cater to diverse IoT needs, balancing coverage, cost, and application requirements.</p></p>
