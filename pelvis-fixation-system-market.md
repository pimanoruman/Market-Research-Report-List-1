<p><p>The Pelvis Fixation System market is categorized into two main types: Internal Fixation Systems and External Fixation Systems. Internal Fixation Systems involve the surgical implantation of devices such as plates and screws within the body to stabilize pelvic fractures internally. In contrast, External Fixation Systems utilize external devices to stabilize pelvic bones through pins or rods anchored outside the body. Both approaches aim to ensure proper alignment and healing of pelvic injuries, catering to different clinical needs and patient conditions.</p></p>
