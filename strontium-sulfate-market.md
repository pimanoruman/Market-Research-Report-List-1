<p><p>Strontium sulfate (SrSO₄) is a white crystalline solid compound composed of strontium, sulfur, and oxygen. Strontium sulfate is also utilized in the oil and gas industry as a weighting agent in drilling fluids due to its high density and chemical stability.</p><p>The Strontium Sulfate Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period.
