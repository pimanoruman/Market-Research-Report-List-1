<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low Sugar Pectin Amidated is categorized into High Methoxyl (HM) and Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin types. HM Pectin typically requires higher sugar content to gel, making it suitable for traditional jams and jellies. In contrast, LM Pectin gelling occurs with lower sugar levels or even sugar substitutes, making it ideal for low-sugar or diet products. Amidated pectin, with its altered structure, enhances gelling capabilities under reduced sugar conditions, appealing to health-conscious consumers seeking flavorful, reduced-sugar options.</p></p>
