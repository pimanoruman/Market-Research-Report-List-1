<p><p>The turbine oils market consists of two primary types: mineral oil-based and synthetic oil-based turbine oils. Mineral oil-based turbine oils are derived from refined petroleum and are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and adequate lubrication properties. In contrast, synthetic oil-based turbine oils are engineered from chemical compounds, offering superior thermal stability, oxidation resistance, and performance in extreme conditions. These synthetic alternatives are increasingly favored in high-efficiency turbines where enhanced lubrication and longevity are critical for optimal performance.</p></p>
