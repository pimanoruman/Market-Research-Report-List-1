<p><p>The Distributed Optical Sensing System market encompasses various types of sensing technologies, including Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS), Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS), and Distributed Vibration Sensing (DVS). DTS uses optical fibers to monitor temperature variations along a cable, facilitating applications in industries like oil and gas. DAS detects acoustic signals for monitoring events or intrusions, while DVS measures vibrations for structural health assessments. Each system capitalizes on fiber optics, offering real-time, continuous monitoring across extensive distances and diverse environments.</p></p>
