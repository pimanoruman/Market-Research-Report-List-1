<p><p>The Electric Traction Wire Rope market is categorized into two main types: Fiber Core and Steel Core. Fiber Core ropes are constructed with a central core made of synthetic or natural materials, providing flexibility and reduced weight, making them suitable for lighter applications. Conversely, Steel Core ropes utilize a steel core for enhanced strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty operations and environments that demand high tensile strength. Both types cater to specific operational needs in electric traction systems.</p></p>
