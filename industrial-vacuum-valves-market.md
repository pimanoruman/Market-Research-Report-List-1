<p><p>The industrial vacuum valves market comprises three main types based on pressure ranges: low vacuum valves, which operate in pressures from atmospheric levels to about 1 torr, typically used in general processing; high vacuum valves, functioning between 1 torr and 10^-3 torr, ideal for applications like semiconductor manufacturing; and ultra-high vacuum valves, working below 10^-3 torr, essential for scientific research and high-precision industries. Each type serves specific needs, ensuring efficient control of gas flow in various industrial processes.</p></p>
