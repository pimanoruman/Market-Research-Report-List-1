<p><p>The at-home hair color and dye market comprises three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dye offers a short-lived color that typically lasts a few washes, ideal for experimenting without long-term commitment. Semi-permanent dye lasts longer, generally up to about 8-12 washes, providing a richer color but gradually fading. Permanent hair dye penetrates the hair shaft for long-lasting results, often requiring a more involved application process and maintenance but delivering vibrant, enduring color transformations.</p></p>
