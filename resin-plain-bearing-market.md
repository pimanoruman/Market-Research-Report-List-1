<p><strong>The Resin Plain Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Sliding Bearing</li><li>Thrust Sliding Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Resin Plain Bearing market includes two primary types: Radial Sliding Bearings and Thrust Sliding Bearings. Radial Sliding Bearings are designed to support radial loads, allowing for smooth rotational motion, making them ideal for applications like electric motors and pumps. Thrust Sliding Bearings, on the other hand, are specifically engineered to handle axial loads and are commonly used in applications such as gear systems and machinery where axial force management is crucial. Both types benefit from the durability and low friction properties of resin materials.</p></p>
