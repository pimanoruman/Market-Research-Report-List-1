<p><p>Electrical Discharge Machines (EDM) utilize controlled electrical discharges to erode material and create precise shapes. The market comprises three main types: Wire Cutting Machines, which use a thin metal wire to cut intricate profiles; Die Sinking EDM, designed for creating molds and cavities by allowing the electrode to sink into the workpiece; and Hole Drilling EDM, which focuses on creating precise holes in hard materials. Each type serves specific applications, contributing to the broader machining and manufacturing landscape.</p></p>
