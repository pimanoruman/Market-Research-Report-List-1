<p><ul><li>Tourniquet Instrument</li><li>Tourniquet Cuffs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tourniquet systems market comprises two primary types: tourniquet instruments and tourniquet cuffs. Tourniquet instruments are devices used to apply controlled pressure to limbs, effectively occluding blood flow during medical procedures, surgeries, or emergencies. Tourniquet cuffs, on the other hand, are specifically designed bands or sleeves that encircle limbs, providing adjustable pressure for hemostasis. Both types are essential in trauma care and surgical settings, improving patient outcomes by reducing blood loss and enhancing procedural efficacy.</p></p>
