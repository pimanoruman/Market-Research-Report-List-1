<p><ul><li>Standard Heat Pipes</li><li>High Conductivity Plates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper heat pipe market encompasses two main types: standard heat pipes and high conductivity plates. Standard heat pipes efficiently transfer heat using a sealed copper structure filled with a liquid that vaporizes and condenses, providing reliable thermal management in various applications. High conductivity plates, on the other hand, feature advanced designs that enhance thermal conductivity, enabling superior heat distribution across surfaces. Both types are crucial in industries such as electronics, aerospace, and renewable energy, where effective heat dissipation is essential for performance and reliability.</p></p>
